About

I am a creator based in Edmonton, Alberta developing integrated system solutions, interactive gaming media, and motion graphics.

In Industrial Design, I'm interested in the processes of which technology works with itself and different stakeholders in aspects of life, such as work and home. Different areas of this are becoming more and more noticeable with the rise of the "Smart Home" and automation like IFTTT.

Games and how they're made have always been a passion to me, and working to make innovative ways to work with games has been a passion throughout my time designing games, whether it's creating a new mechanic or repurposing engines for thing it never was designed for.
